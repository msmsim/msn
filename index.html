Matthew McDowell-Sweet | In Motion

Hi. How's it going? I'm Matt; a reader, writer, product manager, self-taught programmer and researcher. Broadly, I'm interested in complex systems, computing and communication.

The simplest way to reach me is email: matt@thisdomain. I use email for correspondence, not capture. Cold emails are welcome and I'm generally open to agenda-less intro calls; I'm responsive by default and I operate by Crocker's Rules. I'm also reachable on on Warpcast, Twitter and a dynamic set of Discord servers and Slack groups.

Now

Some insight into what I'm up to right now.

Product @ a UK-based, B2B software marketplace startup
    Product at a UK-based, B2B software marketplace (late 2020 - now). Someone once asked me what my particular flavour of product looks like. The answer: team-first, discovery-orientated, paradigmatic, participatory, centaur-ish and agnostic to any specific useful fictions.
Advisory, consulting etc. @ various
    Advising and consulting with various orgs and individuals (mid 2022 - now). Sustained engagements focused on evolving challenges, emerging domains and evergreen issues. A typical arrangement consists of recurring video calls and ongoing asynchronous interactions over an extended period of time. Guises I assume include—but are not limited to—fractal PM, sparring partner, live rubber duck, friendly inquisitor and source of tangential artefacts and insight.
BDFx @ Subset
    Benevolent dictator (July 2023 - now). Multiple years of producing Mag7 have taught me that indexing, distributing and publishing the interesting things one finds on the web is complicated. Subset is designed to change that.
Research @ yet-to-be-named
    Researching new and alternative paradigms of computing and intelligence that are non-discretised and synthesise contemporary understandings of organic, machine and socio-technical systems (late 2021 - now). Thomas Nail's "kinetic case studies", Burgess' "smart spacetime", this processual biology text and a SFI volume on energetics provide pointers while I slouch towards something shareable.
Governance studies @ The Yak Collective
    The Yak Collective "is an online space for collaborative tinkering ... a space where you can work with others on gardening your indie or weekends-and-evenings life in collaboration with others who share some of your priorities / interests." I'm mostly involved with the recurring governance studies group (early 2022 - now) but it's not uncommon to find me weighing in on a Discord thread and enviously side-eyeing other YC activity tracks.
Curation @ The Magnificent Seven
    The Magnificent Seven is my weekly newsletter of curated artefacts (July 2020 - now). It goes out every Sunday at 0700 GMT/BST and contains seven things that elicited a “Whoa!”, a “Wow!”, a “Huh?” or a “Hmmm” from me—mostly text-intensive items like books, articles, blog posts or essays; sometimes projects, graphics, ideas, excerpts, videos, songs, tools, artists, art, creators, software or hardware.
Blogging @ Swell and Cut
    From August 2015 to September 2018 S&C was a daily blog about mastery, strategy and practical philosophy. Now, S&C is where I post infrequent miscellanea and publish the Status of the Wholes, my annual review-slash-roundup.
